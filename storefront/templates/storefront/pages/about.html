About Us

Nexus Marketplace connects communities, businesses, and customers through a trusted, innovative platform. Our mission is to empower local commerce and make shopping smarter for everyone.

Join as a Business

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for local business discovery, fair pricing, and community-driven commerce in 2026 and beyond.

Our Story

Nexus started as a small project to help neighbourhood businesses reach more customers without excessive fees or lock-in. Over time we've grown into a platform focused on fairness, transparency, and community-first commerce.

Our Team

We are a passionate group of technologists, business owners, and community advocates working to make commerce better for all. Our team blends design, engineering, and local market expertise so that the platform works for merchants and shoppers alike.

Core Values

  • Community first — local businesses power the neighbourhood economy.
  • Fair pricing — transparent fees and predictable costs for merchants.
  • Privacy & safety — we protect user data and prioritize secure transactions.
